Our Story

PineCoder FinTech was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between trading strategies and automated execution. We recognized that talented traders across India had profitable ideas but lacked access to the technical expertise needed to bring them to life.

Built by quantitative trading engineers with hands-on experience in automated algorithmic trading platforms, real-time market data systems, and strategy development, we bring institutional-grade technology to retail traders. Our team holds NISM certifications and combines deep market knowledge with cutting-edge cloud infrastructure expertise.

Today, PineCoder FinTech stands as a trusted partner for traders looking to automate their edge. From Pine Script indicators to full-stack trading bots, we deliver clean, reliable code that executes your vision—making algo trading accessible, affordable, and efficient for the Indian trading community.
